Pioneer Woman with a Teething Toddler!

December 5, 2009 by ak

Pioneer Woman was coming to Houston today and my husband and I (both fans) thought we’d throw caution to the wind and go meet her and get her to sign the cookbook we received many weeks ago.  Here were the odds stacked against us:

  1. We were bringing our toddler with us
  2. Our toddler just this past week started waking up at 3a and when we were wondering what was going on he started biting the chair rail on the wall in our dining room and we realized DUH teething.
  3. We were going during the most important time of all times, naptime.  And naptime in our house is most sacred I tell you…because mainly it involves renapping and if said renapping doesn’t occur, all hell breaks loose that night.

But BLAGH we decided to go anyway…I mean it’s Pioneer Woman for land’s sakes!  In Houston!  And what if she was handing out Monkey Muffins?!  I would never forgive myself for passing it up.

So here’s how it went…we showed up to this:

Waited around and said toddler proceeded to scream bloody murder because he was in a grocery store and not in his crib asleep…so after becoming mildly embarrassed when people would stare at us with that “what ARE you doing to that child” look, we decided to chance it and sit down instead of walk around.

So we had about 30 minutes of this, then he had some milk and we got called up to get in line.  So of course he charmed the pants off everyone in line.  A big shoutout to the kind woman behind us who talked to him and got him to face the camera while we were taking pictures.  Thank you!  So anyway, we get up to the front of the line to meet Ree and guess what–toddler turns crazed squirming whining lunatic!  But we did get a good pic!

We had a great time and thanks to Ree/Pioneer Woman for making the trip out and taking the time to take a pic with a squawking small fry.  She also gave us a t-shirt!  SQUEEEE!

DIY Gifts for your Neighbor, Co-Worker, Teacher or somebody like that

December 3, 2009 by ak

Are you looking for a cheap gift to give a neighbor, a co-worker, a teacher, a babysitter or somebody in that realm…you don’t want to spend a ton but you want them to know you care? I’m going to give you a few ideas…

Give them stuff in a jar.  Get a jar, get some cute fabric, and get some raffia to tie the fabric on with and shove one of these ideas in.  You can either put the ingredients for a mix of some sort minus the wet ingredients or put something pre-made (ala nuts). Write or print out the recipe and attach it.

  1. Cowboy Cookies
  2. Nuts:  Sweet (Cinnamon Almonds) or Spicy (Hot & Spicy Peanuts)
  3. Hot Cocoa Mix
  4. Spice Rub
  5. Bean Soup
  6. Dog Biscuit Mix

Thinking something a little more hardcore than nuts, cocoa mix and dog biscuits?  How ’bout a round on you? So…this may not be the one to give a teacher or babysitter especially if they are caring for your children at the time…this might be more of a neighbor/co-worker thing.

Make a homemade mixer and pour it in a pretty bottle, then include whatever libation goes with it in mini bottle form, put them on a mini serving tray (or dessert plate) and tie it up with some cellophane.  Write or print out the drink “recipe” and attach it.

  1. Pomegranate Martini
  2. Margarita
  3. Spiked Hot Cocoa
  4. Spiked Apple Cider

Toddler Books in High Rotation at My House

December 2, 2009 by ak

If you are looking for a gift for a little one on your list, let me tell you….these 5 books are almost at falling apart state because my kiddo LOVES LOVES LOVES them. He carries them around, he brings them to me or my husband to read, he wants to turn the pages, and on and on.

  1. Baby Fix My Car, this book is part of a series of the “Baby Be of Use” books.  They are hysterical.  I’m not sure what draws my son to the car one, but he likes to just carry it around with him.
  2. Five Silly Monkeys, about monkeys jumping on a bed and all their shenanigans that ensue because of it.  My son loves the rhymes and of course the end…you’ll have to read it, I don’t want to ruin it for you.
  3. The Going to Bed Book, just a cute cute book.  This was my son’s first favorite book ever…I can’t even count how many times I’ve read this.
  4. If You See a Kitten, initially you might think this is a girly book but OH NO!  This book has many opportunities to partake in special sound effects and my son LOVES it!
  5. The Pigeon Loves Things That Go, if you haven’t heard of this pigeon series–OMG you MUST!  They are super super funny, very fun to read and have both me, my husband and my son cracking up.  This is just one of the favs, but all of them are great.
