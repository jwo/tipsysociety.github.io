Hump Day Happy Hour: Lemon Drop Martini

April 27, 2011 by sarah

It has been HOTT up in here lately, and doesn’t this sound so refreshing!?!?!?!

  • 1.5 oz Vodka
  • .5 oz Triple Sec
  • .75 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Super-fine sugar
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Mix all ingredients in a cocktail shaker, pour, and serve!
