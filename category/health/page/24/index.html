Hump Day Happy Hour: White Christmas Punch

December 9, 2009 by court

It’s that time to start getting fancy with our cocktails (somewhat)!

Now I know lots of people CRINGE when they hear eggnog (I personally love it). White Christmas Punch is a really nice alternative to mix up for guests who are not extremely fond of just straight Eggnog.

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz eggnog
  • 1/2 oz white chocolate liqueur
  • 1 oz Southern Comfort
  • edible gold flakes or chocolate flakes for garnish

Prep:

  1. Build the ingredients in to snifter glass and stir lightly.
  2. Sprinkle gold or chocolate flakes on top.

You can make this on a much larger scale by pouring larger quantities (be generous with the Southern Comfort!) into a large punch bowl.
