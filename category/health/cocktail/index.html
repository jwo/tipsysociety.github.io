Hump Day Happy Hour: March Madness On Crack

March 23, 2011 by melis

March Madness began last week.  For those of you who don’t know, it is the huge NCAA college basketball tournament. I happened to be in Las Vegas when it began, and let me tell you, those people were definitely on something. Was it crack, or just a shit ton of alcohol, we will never know. What I do know is that it was crazy. Now, I am not a big basketball fan, but this year Houston is the lucky host of the Final Four so I thought I should celebrate that with a cocktail (even though my team lost in the first round…).

1 oz Peach Schnapps
1 oz Triple Sec
1 1/2 oz Vodka
4 oz  Monster Energy Drink
3 oz Orange Gatorade

Mix it all together and serve in a highball glass.

* The recipe actually calls for Amp energy drink but I personally think it is disgusting so I changed it to Monster. If you are anti-energy drink, I imagine this drink would taste good without that ingredient, although you may want to add some soda so its not too strong…

recipe courtesy of drinknation.com

    Hump Day Happy Hour: Sweet Melissa

    March 2, 2011 by melis

    I have yet to try this drink, but I thought I would share it with y’all since it shares my name 🙂 Doesn’t it sound like it should involve a lounge chair, a pool, and the sun… and maybe even a paper umbrella? I can foresee all of these things in my future…

    • 1 1/2 oz. Rum, coconut
    • 1 1/2 oz. Vodka, vanilla
    • 1 splash Cranberry Juice
    • 1 part Orange Juice
    • 1 part Pineapple Juice
    • 1 wedge Lime

    Add rum and vodka to a hurricane glass full of ice. Fill with desired amount of pineapple and orange juice, topping with a splash of cranberry. Squeeze a lime wedge over it and then drop it in. Enjoy!

    Hump Day Happy Hour: Spiked Spring Fever

    February 16, 2011 by court

    I sure as hell have the fever right now.  This drink is absolutely perfect.  This is supposed to be a mocktail, but I’ve spiced it up a bit, Tipsy style.

    • 3/4 oz RealLemon lemon juice
    • 3/4 oz Torani mango syrup
    • 1 1/2 oz Mott’s apple juice
    • 2 oz Volcano “blood” orange juice
    • 1 1/2 oz Absolut vodka

    Add into a cocktail shaker with ice.  Shake well, strain into Collins glassed half filled with ice.  Enjoy and bring on the heat!
