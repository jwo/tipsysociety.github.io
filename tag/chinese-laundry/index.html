Chinese Laundry, Need I Say More?

March 16, 2010 by court

I have always loved this brand for their quirky styles.  Booty lifters, flats, sandals, wedges.  They have it all and when I find a store that carries it they generally catch my eye.

I will also confess that my eyes were caught (hook line and sinker) when I found these stripper heels by Chinese Laundry babies at a garage sale this weekend for 3 dollars!  Wore them today and not only was I four inches taller, I was 4 more units of BADASS.

Heres some of picks from their latest line:

  1. Citrus
  2. Shantel
  3. Bobbi
  4. Malini
  5. Cool it
  6. Morocco
  7. Chat

Nothing can beat my 3.00 find at the garage sale; however, Chinese Laundry’s prices are very conservative–I am talking UNDER 50.00!  Woot Woot!
