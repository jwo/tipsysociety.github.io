Fri, 26 Apr 2013 12:03:38 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Fabulous Footwear Friday http://tipsysociety.com/2011/04/fabulous-footwear-friday-44/ Fri, 29 Apr 2011 19:00:53 +0000 http://tipsysociety.com/?p=8265 I love Reef flip flops, they are super duper comfy!  As I was browsing the other day I ran across these beauties that are adorable but there is more to it….

The beads on the t-strap are handcrafted by displaced women in Uganda and are made of 100% recycled paper.
Part of the Reef Redemption series. Reef incorporates renewable, recycled, and/or organic materials wherever possible – and all without compromising their signature fit, quality, comfort, or style. 1% of all proceeds from the Reef Redemption product series will benefit various environmental and humanitarian causes globally.

Gotta love that!  Looking cute and helping out!
