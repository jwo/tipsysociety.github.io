Tipsy Favs: Body Wash

May 21, 2010 by der

I like to think of myself as a body wash connoisseur. I see a new and interesting scent and sure enough it’s hopped it’s little self right into my shopping cart. So, here are the Tipsy favs for washin those bodies:

  1. Court- Bath & Body Works Japanese Cherry Blossom Shower Gel: My fav, and this is a shocker, is bath and body works Japanese cherry blossom.  Love the smell and it tends to linger just enough, but its light enough that it doesn’t stay on your skin after your bath (or shower for those of you who take them).
  2. AK- Caress Daily Silk Body Wash with White Peach and Silk Blossom: I’ve used this for years, I love the subtle fragrance and that it leaves the skin moisturized and soft.
  3. Sarah- St. Ives Oatmeal and Shea Butter: This body wash is moisturizing, smells fresh, and both my husband and I use it. What’s even better is that you can pick it up at your local drug OR grocery store!
  4. Der- Zest Marathon: I tried this on a whim because the bottle was silver and shiny, but when I opened the cap, man o man was I sold.  This smell is so clean, almost like laundry detergent, and I’m a sucker for laundry detergent smell.
