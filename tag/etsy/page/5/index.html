New Year’s Resolutions: Der 2011

January 4, 2011 by der
  1. I want to finish losing the 8-10 pounds I have remaining of baby weight and get back into working out like I did pre-baby. It was all coming off so fast and then the holidays came and everything seemed to stop in that area.(I swear by Jillian and have been diving into kettle bells with this one)
  2. I am going back to school to become an elementary school teacher. This is something that I have always wanted to do and after having my son, I realized that I really want to have a career that inspires me and makes me want to go to work every day. (cutest backpack ever here)
  3. I want to learn more about photography. I love looking at and taking pictures and always have. I have this camera and I use it regularly, but I know I’m not even close to using it for it’s full potential.
  4. Cherish every moment I have with my son. I find myself saying ” I can’t wait til he can walk or talk, etc” , but I need to not wish away the adorable infant stage he is in now and remain focused on just enjoying every single thing because it really does go by way too quickly. I honestly can see why some women have 10 kids. Not saying I am one of those, but you know what I mean.
