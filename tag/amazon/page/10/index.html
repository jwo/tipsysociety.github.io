Fountain of Youth

August 23, 2010 by ak

Remember when we talked about these moisturizers? I really liked the Aveeno for winter but in the summer wooo man that stuff was a bit THICK.  So I was wanting to try something else that was anti-aging and was talking to my cousin about it and she said that I needed to try this line by Oil of Olay.  That she had been using it and had told everybody in our family about it except me apparently.  I guess she wants me to look all old and wrinkly.  So I bought it and I do really like it.  The moisturizer is light and goes on great under makeup.  The eye cream and night cream, neither of which have ever been on my agenda in terms of skin care, are actually really good.  I haven’t broken out with either of them, which is generally something I worry about with moisturizer stuff.  And the fragrance is very light, nothing overpowering in the least.  Oh and also, I look 10 years younger after just 2 weeks of using these products.*

*that might be a lie

Pampers Extra Protection vs Huggies Overnights

July 8, 2010 by ak

In terms of an overnight diaper we always just by default picked up Huggies Overnights, even though we use Pampers during the day.  I couldn’t tell you how many times we would deal with leakage in the morning, so we ended up getting things called diaper doublers that we would put inside of the Huggies Overnights to absorb even more.  A couple of weeks ago we saw these Pampers Extra Protection diapers and decided why not?  Worth a shot right? So we tried them without the diaper doubler and lo and behold no leakage.  We’ve been using them for a few weeks now and haven’t had any leakage whatsoever.  It’s crazy because they are so much thinner feeling than Huggies and yet, no leakage.  Anybody else a Pampers Extra Protection fan?
