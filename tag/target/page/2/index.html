Curvy Girl Shorts

June 28, 2011 by ak

You know how generally you are classified as either an apple or a pear  in terms of your body shape? I tend to lean more towards a pear but with the boobs too, so I definitely consider myself curvy.  And when you are curvy you definitely have problems finding shorts/pants/jeans that don’t gape open in the waist when they actually fit in the butt.

I was strolling through Target the other day and bought three pairs of these shorts (they come in several different colors but only seem to offer the light khaki color on the website) without trying them on because I was just too damn lazy to.  I figured I could return them if they didn’t fit when I put them on at home.  So I get home and tried them on later that night and hoooo boy did I hit the jackpot on fit! These are a curvy girl’s dream in fit and a great everyday pair of shorts.

Merona Cargo Shorts, Target
