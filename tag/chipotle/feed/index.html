First up: ShakeItPhoto. Turn one of your existing camera pics into a Polaroid or take a polaroid pic with ShakeItPhoto. What’s fun is that you shake your phone to “develop” the pic. (BTW you’re welcome–I resisted making a “shake it like a polaroid” joke)

Next, Look Baby! This app makes my kid very happy and giggle a TON. There are 4 different settings but the two in use the most around here are the Teddy Bear who giggles when you touch his belly and the Bubbles that pop when you catch them. A great distraction!

And lastly, if you are a fan of Chipotle you will love their app. Last weekend I was getting my nails done, texted my hubby and asked if he wanted me to bring home Chipotle for lunch. He suggested the app and I had it ordered, paid for and scheduled for pickup in no time.
