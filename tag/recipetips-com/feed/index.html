Fri, 26 Apr 2013 12:03:38 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.5.2 Tortillas Filled With Goodness http://tipsysociety.com/2010/10/tortillas-filled-with-goodness/ Tue, 12 Oct 2010 19:00:51 +0000 http://tipsysociety.com/?p=5803 One of the girls that regularly attends Keno with us made these for Keno a while back and I couldn’t get enough of these in my belly.  So I made these for the adults at my son’s birthday party.  You can’t go wrong with these, I promise and they take next to no time to make.

Tortilla Rollups
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
16 ounces sour cream
1 package taco seasoning
2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
8 10-inch flour tortillas

Combine cream cheese, sour cream, taco seasoning and cheese. Spread on the flour tortillas and roll them up. Cut 1 inch pieces. You can serve this with salsa, but man I could eat a thousand of them without.
