Corral Boots GIVEAWAY!

June 30, 2010 by ak

We have posted on our love for boots many times in the past.  Most recently was a post in May written by moi regarding all of these colorful boots that we were drooling over.  In an act of internet magicness, Tipsy reader Danielle has hooked us up with the peeps at Langston’s who are so generous to host a giveaway for an ENTIRE PAIR OF CORRAL BOOTS to one lucky winner.

Today is one of those days where I wish I was just a reader of Tipsy Society so I could partake in the awesomeness of this prize.  I mean can you BELIZE it? One whole pair of Corral Boots!  So I need you to think of the sacrifices I make for you.

Go check out Langston’s large selection of Corral boots…just to get your drool on.  Or your engine running.  Or whatever effect boots may have on you… If you need something from Langston’s STAT, head on over now because for today only, Langston’s is giving all Tipsy readers 10% off their entire order.  Just enter promo code TIPSY10OFF.

If you want to enter the giveaway, leave a comment letting us know what boots you drool over.

The Rules

One entry per person. No entries after 5:00 pm Central Time Friday.  Winners will be randomly selected from among entrants. Winners will be announced at 9:00 am Central Time Monday.
