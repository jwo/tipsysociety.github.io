Have your husband bring one home (preferably BEFORE you two come home from the hospital) after Baby Boy has been hanging out in it. Let the dog smell it, leave it on the floor, whatever. This will help the dog get used to the scent so he doesn’t charge at the baby like, “What’s this new thing?” with too much enthusiasm. My parents did this with Evan and he and their dog have generally been fine since day one. (The dog was unimpressed with the 2am crying, but fair enough, right?)
